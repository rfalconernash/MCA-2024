Week[2] Music as Notation

This week we focused on how music notation is processed by computers and programs such as Musescore. For this week, we put a pdf from our chosen theme into Musescore to analyse and turn into playable audio. I chose a section of a romantic piano piece called Pensee Lyrique by Sergei Bortkiewicz to upload into musescore. You can access this piece on musescore here

Some problems that Musescore had with translating from a pdf into Musescore format was: Not adding the title and composer, no pedal indications, incorrectly inputting crescendo/diminuendo markings and mistaking some naturals as sharps - these all had to be manually changed. Most of these issues were ones that we saw as a group on Tuesday across a variety of genres and sources - rock, pop, classical.
